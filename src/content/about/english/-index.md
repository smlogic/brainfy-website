In a small but dynamic tech environment, two entrepreneurs Alexey and Ilya joined forces to build a company aimed at radically transforming the world of engineering automation. Gaining recognition for their ability to significantly simplify and accelerate engineering processes, they embraced an ambitious approach driven by innovative solutions. As a result, they quickly emerged as notable players in the market, providing revolutionary tools for any business striving to optimize its engineering systems.

#### 2007 | Industrial Process Control Systems
This is where it all began. We deliver reliable solutions for programming industrial machines and equipment. Our goal: maximum efficiency, zero downtime, and a noticeable increase in production output.

#### 2015 | HVAC Automation (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning)
The next step was intelligent climate management in buildings. We implemented innovative control and monitoring systems that not only improve comfort but, most importantly, reduce operating costs and boost energy efficiency.

#### 2017 | Smart Lighting Control Systems
We expanded our portfolio with lighting optimization technologies. We implement systems that maintain perfect conditions while helping to lower energy bills and providing full control over the infrastructure.

#### 2018 | Building Management Systems (BMS)
We brought it all together. We introduced modern solutions for the integration of all engineering systems within a facility. We focus on advanced, sustainable technologies that transform traditional buildings into smart ecosystems, ready for the challenges of the future.
